Privacy Policy - ReadItDown Mobile

Effective July 3, 2026

ReadItDown is a markdown viewer for public GitHub repositories. It is designed to work without collecting anything about you.

Data we collect

None. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data.

What stays on your device

Your favorite and recently opened repositories are saved only in the app's local storage on your device. They never leave it, and they are deleted when you uninstall the app.

Network requests

When you open a repository, the app requests its file list and file contents directly from GitHub (api.github.com and raw.githubusercontent.com). These requests are made anonymously, without any account or identifier, and are subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement. The app makes no other network requests.

External links

Links in markdown files open in your device's browser or an in-app browser view. Pages you visit that way are governed by the privacy policies of those websites.

Children

The app is not directed at children and, since it collects no data, collects no data from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be published at this address with an updated effective date.

Contact

Questions or concerns: open an issue at github.com/khanhnd61-vr/readitdown.
